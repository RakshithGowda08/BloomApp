Pregnancy Test Guide

Home Pregnancy Test Kit

Home pregnancy tests detect the presence of human chorionic gonadotropin (hCG) in your urine. This hormone is produced during pregnancy and can be detected as early as 10 days after conception.

For best results, test with your first morning urine when hCG levels are most concentrated.

Step-by-Step Testing Procedure:

1

Prepare the Test

Remove the test stick from its sealed wrapper. Make sure you have a clean, dry container if you're collecting urine.

2

Collect Urine Sample

Either hold the test stick in your urine stream for 5-10 seconds or dip it into a collected urine sample for 5-10 seconds.

3

Wait for Results

Place the test stick on a flat surface and wait for the recommended time (usually 3-5 minutes). Do not read results after 10 minutes.

4

Interpret Results

Positive: Two lines appear (even if one is faint)
Negative: Only one line appears in the control area
Invalid: No lines appear or only the test line appears
